I am a Ph.D. Student in Electrical Engineering at Arizona State University in Tempe, AZ, USA under the direction of Dr. Visar Berisha and Dr. Julie Liss. I completed my M.S. (2013) and B.S. (2010) in Electrical Engineering from the University of California - Los Angeles, CA, USA.

My primary research interests include Speech and Natural Language Processing, in particular with respect to clinically relevant applications.
