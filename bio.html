Biography of Rohit Voleti

Rohit Voleti is a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at Arizona State University (ASU). He began his Ph.D. in Fall 2016 and joined the Brain and Behavior Analytics Lab under Dr. Berisha and Dr. Liss in January 2018.

Prior to his time at ASU, he worked in the medical device industry in San Diego, CA for Becton Dickinson (BD)/CareFusion as a Systems Engineer. He was also previously an Electrical Engineering Intern at Advanced Bionics in Valencia, CA. Rohit completed his B.S. in Electrical Engineering (Biomedical Option) in 2010 and M.S. Electrical Engineering (Systems & Signals Area) in 2013 from UCLA.
